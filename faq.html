What is the dress code?

Cocktail attire. This means a nice dress or a suit. Per venue rules, please refrain from wearing anything with fine glitter. We also ask that you do not wear white or any shades of white.

Can I bring my kid(s)?

No. This is an adult only event. We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home so you can celebrate with us.

Can I take and post pictures of the wedding on social media?

Absolutely! We have the wedding hashtag #ToDioniseFor so all of the photos can be in the same place! We’d love to see that you had a good time!

I still have questions, how can I contact you?

If you don’t have either of our numbers feel free to email me at albussar@gmail.com and I’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Where should I park?
